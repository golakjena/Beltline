Bariatric Clinic in Atlanta and Newnan Georgia

When you're ready to take control of your health and achieve your weight loss goals, we're here to help.

Our modern, fully-accredited bariatric clinic & hernia treatment center caters to patients in Atlanta and Newnan, throughout Georgia, across the United States, and beyond who are seeking surgical treatment for chronic obesity or hernia repair.

What Makes Beltline Bariatric Unique? Why our patient's choose us?

Our team of compassionate and professional support staff ensure you are provided with the best care. Once you start your surgical process with us, we're sure you'll agree that Beltline Bariatric is a different kind of bariatric clinic.

Our comprehensive medical weight loss and hernia treatment services include pre-operative education sessions, one-on-one surgical consultations, and ongoing support. Our team of nutritional experts, exercise physiologists, counselors, and nurses have advanced training and experience in bariatric medicine.

We're proud to be the bariatric clinic of choice among patients who want access to comprehensive, full-service bariatric care that goes above and beyond what you might expect from a weight loss surgeon.

Our team of committed, compassionate bariatric medicine specialists is led by Dr. Charles Procter, Jr, a dedicated surgeon who has focused his entire career on helping people who struggle with chronic obesity.

Dr. Procter is one of the most sought-after bariatric surgeons in the Southeastern United States. He is a pioneer in the use of robotic technologies to improve patient outcomes from procedures such as Roux-en-Y gastric bypass and sleeve gastrectomy. Dr. Proctor also specializes in the treatment and repair of acquired and congenital hernias.

Dr. Procter has a 5-star rating on Vitals.com, and along with his team, has achieved exceptional patient outcomes and satisfaction rates.

Centrally Located in Atlanta and Newnan, Georgia

We perform minimally-invasive bariatric procedures and hernia repairs at Piedmont Atlanta Hospital, Piedmont Newnan Hospital, and Northside Hospital in Atlanta, making is easy for you to access cutting-edge bariatric and hernia care in world class medical facilities located close to hotels, airports, and area attractions.

Our locations make it easy for local and out-of-town patients to access our bariatric clinic services. We have a specialized medical tourism program in place designed to support patients who travel to Atlanta for weight loss surgery.
