Get Rid of Excess Fat

Excess fat is the broad term applied to various problem areas where undesirable or unwanted fat is present. Some of the common terms given to excess fat include love handles, saddlebags, paunches and more. In many cases, excess fat can be treated.

What is Excess Fat?

Excess fat can affect nearly anyone, even those who maintain proper diet and exercise habits. Excess fat is typically visible in a sagging, pocket-like way that can occur in almost every part of the body.
Types of excess fat include:

  • Belly fat (stomach fat)
  • Back fat
  • Neck fat
  • Thigh fat
  • Buttocks
  • Arm fat

Some people simply struggle with one area of their body, and that area may not respond to traditional fat reduction methods such as diet and exercise. Excess fat is referred to by many other names, some of which are hurtful or derogatory. Some common terms for excess fat include:

  • Love handles
  • Saddlebags
  • Paunches
  • Belly
  • Jowls
  • Gut
  • Spare tire
  • Fat bulges

Thankfully, in many cases, these issues can be treated.

How to Get Rid of Excess Fat?

There are numerous excess fat treatment options available. They range from minor to major, much like any other treatment category. They are often referred to as fat reduction procedures. These procedures are not a substitute for diet and exercise, which can eliminate much of the fat from the body.

Instead, these procedures are designed to target the areas of problem fat that cause many people self-confidence and body image issues. Different types of fat reduction treatment include:

  • Liposuction
  • Non-invasive fat reduction
  • Non-surgucal fat reduction
  • Laser fat reduction
  • Ultrasound fat reduction
  • Cold therapy fat reduction

There are a wide variety of options available, only a professional can help determine which one is right for your needs and body. Just as each person is different, two people may have varying results with the same treatment option.
